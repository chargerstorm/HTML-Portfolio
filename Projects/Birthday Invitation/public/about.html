About Me
Greetings! I'm Octavio Mejia, and my journey has been a fascinating blend of technology and creativity. Growing up, the allure of computers always intrigued me—the sheer wonder of how they function and the vast expanse of the Internet captivated my imagination. Each unique website I encountered fueled my curiosity further, leading me down the path of information systems.

Passion meets profession:
My academic endeavors crystallized my career aspiration: web development. But my interest didn't stop there. Photography has always held a special place in my heart. Since 2018, armed with my first camera and guided by a mentor, I've delved into portrait photography, capturing moments frozen in time.

Proud Moments:
Two projects stand out in my academic journey: crafting a minesweeper game in C++ and designing a simple computer (RSC) using logic gates in Logisim. While they posed challenges, from intricate debugging to seeking guidance, the joy of seeing them come to fruition was unparalleled. These experiences taught me resilience and the power of perseverance.

Networking and Growth:
I had the pleasure of participating in HackMT during my university days—a thrilling 36-hour sprint of ideation, creation, and collaboration. Interacting with peers from MTSU and beyond, alongside industry professionals, enriched my perspective and fueled my passion for innovation.

Beyond Pixels and Code:
When I step away from screens and lenses, you'll find me immersed in a good book, especially those delving into the mysteries of space. And when the need for adrenaline kicks in, you might spot me running or hitting the gym, embracing the harmony of mind and body.

Looking Ahead:
On the horizon of the next decade, I envision myself thriving as a web developer at a company that resonates with my values. With each keystroke and click, I'm crafting a future where passion meets purpose.
